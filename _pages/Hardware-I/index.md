# Hardware-I Course Materials

This section contains comprehensive course materials for Hardware-I, covering fundamental concepts in electrical engineering, electronics, and microcontroller systems for IoT applications.

## Course Modules

### [1 - Basic Electrical Theory](1%20-%20Basic%20Electrical%20Theory/)
Fundamental principles of electricity, voltage, current, and basic electrical components.

### [2 - Electric Circuits](2%20-%20Electric%20Circuits/)
Circuit analysis, Ohm's law, and basic circuit design principles.

### [3 - Fundamental Electronics I](3%20-%20Fundamental%20Electronics%20I/)
Introduction to electronic components, diodes, transistors, and basic electronic circuits.

### [4 - Electronics for IoT](4%20-%20Electronics%20for%20IoT/)
Specialized electronic components and circuits used in IoT device development.

### [5 - Introduction to Microcontrollers](5%20-%20Introduction%20to%20Microcontrollers/)
Microcontroller architecture, programming, and interfacing with sensors and actuators.

### [6 - Work Health and Safety](6%20-%20Work%20Health%20and%20Safety/)
Safety protocols and best practices for working with electrical and electronic systems.

## Learning Objectives

By completing this course, students will:
- Understand fundamental electrical and electronic principles
- Learn to analyze and design basic circuits
- Gain practical knowledge of microcontroller systems
- Develop skills in IoT hardware development
- Apply safety protocols in electronic work environments

## Course Structure

Each module includes:
- Chapter notes with detailed explanations
- Exercise solutions for practice problems
- Extra questions for extended learning
- Additional objectives for advanced study

## Laboratory Components

This course includes hands-on laboratory work involving:
- Circuit construction and testing
- Electronic component characterization
- Microcontroller programming and interfacing
- IoT device prototyping
