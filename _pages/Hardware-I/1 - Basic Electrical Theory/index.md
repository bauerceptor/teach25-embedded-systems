# Chapter 1: Basic Electrical Theory

## Overview
This chapter introduces fundamental electrical concepts essential for understanding electronic circuits and IoT hardware systems.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand fundamental electrical quantities (voltage, current, resistance)
- Learn Ohm's law and its applications
- Explore basic electrical components and their properties
- Understand power calculations in electrical circuits
- Apply electrical theory to simple circuit analysis

## Key Topics Covered
- Electric charge and current flow
- Voltage and potential difference
- Resistance and conductance
- Ohm's law and its applications
- Electrical power and energy
- Basic electrical components (resistors, capacitors, inductors)
- Series and parallel circuit configurations
- Kirchhoff's voltage and current laws
- AC and DC fundamentals
- Electrical safety principles

## Prerequisites
- Basic mathematics (algebra and trigonometry)
- Physics fundamentals
- Scientific notation and unit conversions

## Laboratory Activities
- Measuring voltage, current, and resistance
- Verifying Ohm's law experimentally
- Building simple series and parallel circuits
- Using multimeters and basic test equipment
