# Chapter 3: Fundamental Electronics I

## Overview
This chapter introduces fundamental electronic components and circuits, covering semiconductors, diodes, transistors, and basic amplifier circuits essential for IoT hardware development.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand semiconductor physics and properties
- Learn about diode characteristics and applications
- Explore transistor types and their operating principles
- Design and analyze basic amplifier circuits
- Apply electronic components in practical circuit designs

## Key Topics Covered
- Semiconductor materials and properties
- PN junction theory and diode characteristics
- Diode applications (rectifiers, clippers, clampers)
- Bipolar Junction Transistors (BJTs)
- Field Effect Transistors (FETs)
- Transistor biasing and operating points
- Small signal amplifier analysis
- Common emitter, base, and collector configurations
- Frequency response of amplifiers
- Electronic switches and digital circuits

## Prerequisites
- Electric Circuits (Chapter 2)
- Basic mathematical skills (calculus helpful)
- Understanding of AC and DC circuit analysis

## Laboratory Activities
- Diode characteristic measurements and testing
- Transistor biasing and amplifier construction
- Amplifier frequency response measurements
- Electronic circuit simulation and design
- Component testing and characterization
